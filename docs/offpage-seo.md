# Off-Page SEO — Mexico Backlink & Visibility Strategy

**Target:** Mexican professionals searching for English tutoring
**Goal:** Drive backlinks, brand mentions, and direct traffic

---

## 🏆 Quick Wins (Do These First)

### 1. Superprof.mx — HIGHEST PRIORITY
https://www.superprof.mx
- Create a tutor profile — FREE
- Highest domain authority tutoring directory in Mexico
- Students actively search here daily
- List: $400/hr single, $300/hr 10-pack, "Clases de inglés con hablante nativo de EE.UU."

### 2. Google My Business
- Set up service-area business targeting Mexico City / CDMX
- Even without a physical address, you can set "CDMX" as service area
- Critical for local search visibility

### 3. Facebook Page
"English Tutoring Mexico — Mauricio Tellez"
- Post short pronunciation tips in Spanish
- Share student win stories (with permission)
- Link to calendly for free intro calls
- Join groups: "Aprender Inglés México", "Clases de Inglés CDMX"

### 4. LinkedIn Profile Optimization
Headline should be:
> Native US English Speaker 🇺🇸 | Helping Mexican Professionals Succeed in English | Software Engineer
- Post bilingual content about English for tech professionals
- Tag #InglésParaMexicanos #InglésProfesional
